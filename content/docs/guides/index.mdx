## What is Sqala?

Sqala is a payment processor that enables you to:

- Process payments with credit cards, PIX, and boleto.
- Manage subscriptions and recurring payments.
- Get detailed reports and analytics.
- Reduce payment friction and increase conversion rates with our routing system.

## How to get started

To get started, you need to create an account and get your credentials. 

Once you have your credentials, head over to the [Authentication](/docs/guides/authenticate) guide to learn how to authenticate with our API.


If you have any questions, please refer to the [FAQ](https://www.sqala.tech) or contact us at [support@sqala.tech](mailto:support@sqala.tech).
